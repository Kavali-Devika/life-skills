### Prevention of Sexual Harassment

### 1: What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
    
        - Making sexual jokes, comments, or remarks that make others uncomfortable

        - Asking personal or inappropriate questions about someone’s private life

        - Sharing explicit videos, photos, or links without consent

        - Passing suggestive comments through phone calls, chats, or social media

        - Standing too close or invading someone’s personal space unnecessarily

        - Promising benefits or threatening consequences in exchange for personal favors


### 2: What would you do if you face or witness such behaviour?


        - Stay calm and ensure your safety first

        - Inform a trusted senior, manager, or PoSH representative immediately

        - Keep records of all communication related to the incident

        - Do not ignore the situation or assume it will stop on its own

        - Offer emotional support to the victim if you are a witness

        - Cooperate fully during any official investigation
